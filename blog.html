about us

we are a non-governmental organization that provides harm reduction equipment, counselling and rehabilitative services to persons affected by drug abuse.

We carry out overdose prevention ensuring that drug users do not lose their lives.

Beneficiaries of our program also gain skills like baking and crafts

Wick

He was a war veteran who had survived several tours to an active war zone. He had severe PTSD and had acquired a taste for hard drugs like cocaine in order to stun his emotions. It took him around a year to fully get over his addiction. The harm reduction centre paid a key part in his journey by providing him with the care and counselling he so much needed.

Amelia

She was the daughter of a mafia boss. Her comfortable life ended when her father was arrested and all his assets frozen. She found herself in the wrong crowd and found herself headed down the path of drug abuse. The center was her last hope. it saved her from an untimely death.

Rocky

He is easily the oldest member at our center. Having been plagued with chronic diseases in the later stages of his life, he resulted to drugs to dull the pain. He has no desire to quit. His illness is too far gone to hope for treatment. the center's main objective with people in his situation is to prevent an overdose.

Our mission

To save lives at all costs
